Soul Nomad Speedrun

Soul Nomad Speedrun WR (Former)

This may not be a traditional e-portfolio project but I am extremely proud of my speedrun of Soul Nomad for the PS2. This is not a well known game and in fact before I did this run the only "speedrun" that existed was 10 hours long.
Scouring through decade old GameFAQS guides and forums along with some practice with the game I devised a route and strategies for every mission in the game and practiced down to a time of 3 hours and 26 minutes.
Later on I came in contact with a Japanese runner known on youtube as holyangelnyanko who I collaborated with and he took the run down to two and a half hours.
